Danny Ramirez

Danny Ramirez (born September 17, 1992) is an American actor. He is best known as Wes in The Gifted, Mario Martinez in On My Block, Joaquin Torres / Falcon in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, and Lt. Mickey "Fanboy" Garcia in Top Gun: Maverick (2022). Ramirez portrays Manny in The Last of Us (2025).
